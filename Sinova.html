Sinova Group, an Asian-Canadian private equity firm, is a leading platform which focuses on thedistribution and trading of foods, commodities and technologies from throughout the world. The company is lead by an experienced team of specialists in the food industry, construction, mining/oil & gas, investments and commodities trading.

Sinova has embarked upon a rapid and successful expansion campaign of its investment and export activities across the world. Our trading division has also focused on three major areas: food distribution, bulk trade, and expanding investment in Africa.
